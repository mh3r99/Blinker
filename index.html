WHAT IS BLINKER

World-first fast, decentralized and anonymous crypto/fiat trading platform with low fees

With the BLINKER platform any trader across the globe can trade crypto/fiat pairs with savings of up to 90% on fees and enjoy a passive yield $BLINKER token farming

Token mechanisms

$BLINKER token is based on three core functions

Yield generation

5% of every $BLINKER transaction gets re-distributed instantly to all $BLINKER token holders. It automatically appears in your wallet. You can enjoy a passive yield farming mechanism without the explicit need of claiming your token and save on future trading fees.

Ever-growing liquidity pool

5% of every $BLINKER transaction is automatically added to the PancakeSwap $BLINKER-BNB liquidity pool. This creates an unbreakable price floor and makes trading fees lower for each day.

Gas

$BLINKER token is used as a gas in every crypto/fiat trade on our platform. This creates everyday-growing increasing demand.
